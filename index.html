Description

Organic Carrot Oil is 100% natural and certified as organic, as well as being renowned for its softening and antioxidant properties. This organic plant oil produced by the Daucus carota sativa plant is known for being the richest in carotene, especially beta-carotene.

Its wealth of provitamin A means it gives skin an instant natural healthy glow. Organic Carrot Oil's antioxidant properties help boost the skin's defence mechanisms, leaving it soft and comfortable. The oil is suitable for use on both face and body.

For an instant natural healthy glow every day, mix a few drops of Organic Carrot Oil into your day cream or body milk.
